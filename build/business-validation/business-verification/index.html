Business Verification Guide

Streamline KYB processes with instant, authoritative business intelligence.

Business registrations, also known as taxpayer registrations, tax identification numbers, and other terms, are the primary legal identifiers for companies in Latin America and Europe. These records provide information such as the company name, address, type of entity, registration date, and other relevant details.

With Verifik, you can check and validate business records in seconds through automated analysis, access to official sources (depending on the country) and anti-fraud technology designed for KYB processes.

In the digital age, this data is essential for:

  • Business verification
  • Digital registration
  • Fraud prevention
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Electronic signatures and contracts
  • Access to financial services, insurance, education, telecommunications, and more

Comprehensive Business Intelligence​

A business registration number is more than an identifier; it unlocks a company's complete legal profile. By querying a business identifier, Verifik provides access to critical data points, including:

  • Legal Entity Verification: Confirm company name, registration date, legal status, and entity type.
  • Tax & Compliance Status: Validate tax identification numbers, active registrations, and regulatory standing.
  • Corporate Structure: Access information about business type, capital structure, and authorized activities.
  • Administrative Data: Verify registered addresses, contact information, and administrative status.

Note: Data availability varies by country and specific registry regulations.

Global Coverage & Regional Terminology​

A business registration is an official identifier issued by government authorities to validate legal entities and, in some countries, individual taxpayers. In Latin America and Europe, these registrations represent the primary mechanism for officially identifying, consulting, and verifying companies.

Although each country uses different names and formats—such as RUC, NIT, CUIT, CNPJ, CIF, or EIN—they all serve the same function: to certify the legal and tax existence of an entity before public and private bodies.

Verifik standardizes these regional differences, handling local formats, historical variations, and jurisdiction-specific structures to facilitate KYB processes, compliance, and international business validation.

Regional Business Identifier Guide​

Different countries use different terms and formats for business identification. Verifik unifies these under one search parameter:

Identifiers by country:

  • RUC (Registro Único de Contribuyente): Panama, Paraguay, Peru
  • CUIT (Clave Única de Identificación Tributaria): Argentina
  • NIT (Número de Identificación Tributaria): Bolivia, Colombia
  • CNPJ (Cadastro Nacional de Pessoas Jurídicas): Brazil
  • RUT (Rol Único Tributario): Chile
  • NITE (Número de Identificación Tributaria Especial): Costa Rica
  • FME (Folio Mercantil Electrónico): Mexico
  • NIF (Número de Identificación Fiscal): Spain
