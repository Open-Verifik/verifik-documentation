# SENA Certificates

Information about certificates from the Servicio Nacional de Aprendizaje (SENA) in Colombia.

## Description

SENA certificates are documents that certify the training and education received through the Servicio Nacional de Aprendizaje in Colombia.

## Features

- **Certificate Verification**: Verification of authenticity of SENA certificates
- **Training Information**: Access to information about training programs
- **Validation**: Certificate validation process
