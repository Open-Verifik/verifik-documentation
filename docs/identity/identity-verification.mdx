# Identity Verification

**Streamline KYC processes with instant, authoritative identity intelligence.**

Identity verification is the foundation of trust in digital transactions, financial services, and regulatory compliance. With Verifik, you can instantly validate a person's identity by simply querying their government-issued identification number. Our platform connects directly to official government registries across multiple countries, delivering accurate, fraud-proof data in seconds.

## Comprehensive Identity Intelligence

An identity document number is more than an identifier; it unlocks a person's complete legal profile. By querying an identification number, Verifik provides access to critical data points, including:

- **Identity Confirmation:** Verify name, date of birth, nationality, and legal status.
- **Document Validation:** Confirm document authenticity, validity, and expiration status.
- **Biographic Data:** Access registered addresses, contact information, and demographic details.
- **Compliance Status:** Validate eligibility for services, age verification, and regulatory requirements.

*Note: Data availability varies by country and specific registry regulations.*

## Global Coverage & Regional Terminology

Identity identification systems vary significantly across Latin America, Europe, and North America. Verifik normalizes these differences, handling regional formats, special document types, and historical updates seamlessly.

We support validation across **Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico, Panama, Paraguay, Peru, Spain, United States, Uruguay, and Venezuela**.
