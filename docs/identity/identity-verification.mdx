# Identity Verification

Business registrations, also known as taxpayer registrations, tax identification numbers, and other terms, are the primary legal identifiers for companies in Latin America and Europe. These records provide information such as the company name, address, type of entity, registration date, and other relevant details.

With Verifik, you can check and validate business records in seconds through automated analysis, access to official sources (depending on the country) and anti-fraud technology designed for KYC/KYB processes.

In the digital age, this data is essential for:

- Business verification
- Digital registration
- Fraud prevention
- Regulatory compliance
- Electronic signatures and contracts
- Access to financial services, insurance, education, telecommunications, and more.

## Type of Business Records

A Business Registry is an official record issued by a government authority (Latin American or European) that uniquely identifies a taxpayer (citizen) or legal entity (company) in that country. In Latin American countries, this is the primary way to accredit and verify a business.

The business registration or identifier is generally a series of numbers. Each country in the region has assigned a different name to this identifier.
