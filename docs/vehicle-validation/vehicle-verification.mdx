# Vehicle Verification

**Ensure compliance and security with real-time vehicle background checks.**

Vehicle verification is the cornerstone of trust in the automotive, logistics, and insurance industries. With Verifik, you can instantly validate a vehicle's legal status, ownership history, and technical specifications by simply querying its license plate. Our platform connects directly to official transit authorities across multiple countries, providing you with accurate, fraud-proof data in seconds.

## Comprehensive Vehicle Intelligence

A license plate is more than just an identifier; it is the key to a vehicle's complete history. By querying a registration number, Verifik grants you access to critical data points, including:

- **Ownership Verification:** Confirm the current owner's identity and view past transfer history.
- **Legal & Administrative Status:** Check for active theft reports, liens, embargoes, or impound orders.
- **Technical Specifications:** Validate make, model, color, year, and VIN to ensure the physical vehicle matches its documentation.
- **Compliance:** Verify mandatory insurance status and technical inspection validity.

*Note: Data availability varies by country and specific registry regulations.*

## Global Coverage & Terminology

Vehicle identification systems vary by region. Verifik normalizes these differences, handling regional formats, special categories (diplomatic, public transport), and historical plate updates seamlessly.

We support validation across **Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Ecuador, Mexico, Paraguay, Peru, and the United States**.
