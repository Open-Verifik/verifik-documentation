## Q2 2026 - Scale & Integration

- **Migration:** Full customer transition to $ZNS token payment model.
- **SmartBackgroundCheck Premium Launch:** Continuous background check monitoring across multiple databases (FBI, Interpol, OFAC, DEA, Europol, country-specific). Real-time alerts for new records, risk scoring, and compliance reporting. Tiered pricing structure.
- **Dynamic Risk Scoring System:** Real-time risk calculation engine that evaluates users based on behavior, geography, transaction patterns, and historical data. Continuously updates risk scores to reflect evolving threats and compliance requirements.
- **Zero-Biometric KYC Premium Product:** Privacy-preserving identity verification using zero-knowledge proofs. Users can complete KYC verification without ever storing raw biometric data on servers. The system generates cryptographic proofs that validate identity matches while maintaining full privacy compliance. Enables regulatory-compliant verification for financial services, DeFi protocols, and other regulated industries without the privacy risks of traditional biometric storage. Launched as premium product with pricing premium.
- **Proof of Address Verification:** Automated verification of utility bills, bank statements, and government documents. OCR extraction and validation with multi-language support for global compliance requirements.
- **Enhanced Audit Trail:** Immutable audit logs with comprehensive compliance reporting and export capabilities for regulatory reviews and inspections.
- **x402 Protocol Monetization:** Launch agent marketplace with transaction fees on micro-payments, agent registration fees, and enterprise agent solutions. Enable $ZNS token staking benefits for fee reduction.
- **T-REX Protocol:** On-chain identity layer integration with Zelf technology.
- **Security Audits:** Comprehensive external audits of all token contracts.
